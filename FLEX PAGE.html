MY FLEX

I would like to share a bit about my achievements. During my elementary days, I initially began playing soccer just for fun. However, when the coach recognized my potential in the sport, I transitioned into taking soccer more seriously. During tournaments, I found myself consistently excited to face opponents. Regardless of the outcome, whether it was a win or a loss, it didn't matter to me. What mattered most was enjoying the experience and having fun on the field!
